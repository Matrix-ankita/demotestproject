A TIMELESS LOCATION

A hidden gem in the busy heart of Milan, Gioia is nestled next to The Gran Melia Hotel Cordusio, just a few steps behind the stunning Piazza Duomo.

The experience of visiting Gioia Pasticceria is a moment of indulgence in a small yet refined ambiance, a comfortable lounge where every customer feels truly special.

REINVENTING THE ART OF PASTRY

Gioia loves the art of classic pastry, and goes beyond the boundaries of tradition, embracing new trends and tastes. That’s how the “Chic” collection of desserts was born, a modern interpretation of classic haute pastry, where creativity combines with contemporary flavors to create unique and astonishing creations. All products are made with unexpected flavor combinations that perfectly marry tradition and modernity.
